Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this focaccia appetizer tropped with sweer caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favourite holiday starter!

featured in:

Fresh Caranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingridients

For the focaccia:
  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan
For the topping:
  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife is desireed, tjough it's not necesseray)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
